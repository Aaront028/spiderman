Spider-man

Time line of Spider-Man's comic books since 1998:

  • May 10, 1998 - Spider-Man fights crime as other identities.
  • Jan 10, 1999 - Webspinners Tales of Spider-Man#1
  • Jan 10, 1999 - Peter Parker:Spider-Man#1
  • Jan 10, 1999 - Issue number reset
  • Jan 10, 2000 - Mary Jane's plane blows up
  • Jan 10, 2001 - Spider-Man kidnapped by the Green Goblin
  • Jun 10, 2001- Spider-Man Tangled Web#1
  • Jun 10, 2001 - First appearance of Morlun
  • Jun 10, 2001 - First appearance of Ezekiel
  • Dec 10, 2001 - September 11th issue
  • Feb 10, 2002 - May talks to Peter about him being Spider-Man
  • Apr 10, 2003 - Peter and MJ get back together
  • Jun 10, 2004 - Marvel Knights: Spider-Man#1e
  • Jan 10, 2005 -The New Avengers #1
  • Jun 10, 2005 - Peter, Mary-Jane, and Aunt May Move into Stark towers
  • Dec 10, 2005 - Friendly Neighborhood Spider-Man#1
  • Feb 10, 2006 - Peter Parker dies(sorta)
  • Apr 10, 2006 - IronMan makes Spidey a new suit
  • Jul 10, 2006 - Super Hero Civil War begins
  • Aug 10, 2006 - Spider-Man unmasks
  • Nov 10, 2007 - Spidey unwrites his history by dealing with the Devil
